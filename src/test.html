Embark on an exquisite day tour of Ha Long Bay aboard the esteemed 5-star Hercules Premium Cruise. Begin your adventure exploring Sung Sot Cave, renowned for its awe-inspiring stalactites and stalagmites. Throughout the journey, knowledgeable guides provide insights into the bay's geological marvels and cultural significance. This tour promises an unforgettable blend of luxury, relaxation, and exploration in one of Vietnam's most iconic destinations.
